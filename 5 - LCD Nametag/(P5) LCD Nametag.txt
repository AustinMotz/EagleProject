Goals:
1. Learn how i2C communication works
2. Display your name on the LCD
3. Make the LCD scroll with a for loop


Materials: 

 
 4 - Male to female wires
 1 - LCD Display
 - Arduino
 - USB Cable
 - Breadboard

Build:

Take female to male wires and make the connections listed below. The pins are on the back left side.

GND -> GND
VCC -> 5v
SDA -> A4
SCL -> A5
