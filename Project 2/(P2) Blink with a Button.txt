Goals:
1. Make the LED turn on when the button is pressed
2. Make the LED turn off when the button is pressed

Challenge:

 - Push the button and have the LED turn on for 5 seconds



Materials: 

 - 1 220 resistor
 - 1 10K resistor
 - 1 LED
 - 1 Push Button
 - 5 Jumper Wires
 - 1 Arduino Uno
 - 1 USB Cable
 - 1 Breadboard
