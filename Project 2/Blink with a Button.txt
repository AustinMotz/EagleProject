Goals:
1. Make the LED turn on when the button is pressed
2. Make the LED turn off when the button is pressed
3. Push the button and have the LED turn on for 5 seocnds 

Challenge:

 - Add another LED and make the LEDs switch lighting states when the button is pressed


Materials: 

 - 220 ohm resistor
 - 10 Kohm resistor
 - LED
 - Arduino
 - 5 Wires
 - USB Cable
 - Push Button
 - Breadboard
